The Gathering — Live Animation

Watch the chairs pull in at staggered times, like friends arriving at a table. When everyone's seated, it becomes the sun.

Version 2: Random Arrival Order

Chairs arrive in the order: 3 → 1 → 5 → 2 → 6 → 4 (like friends actually showing up)

What's Happening

  • Staggered arrival: Each chair slides in at different times (not synchronized)
  • Table brightens: The center circle gets brighter as more friends arrive
  • The reveal: When the last chair lands, you see it — it's a sun!
  • Sound idea: Gentle chair-sliding sounds at each arrival

The Metaphor

The static logo is the sun. But when you see the chairs pull in, you understand: friends gathering at a table creates warmth. The sun isn't just a sun — it's the feeling of everyone finally being together.

My Take

Option A (Circles) is the clearest "people around a table" read. Simple, friendly, universal.


Option D (Ovals) adds subtle human warmth — the shapes feel more like figures leaning in to the conversation.


Option E (Dashes) is the most abstract but scales perfectly and really captures that sun-ray energy.


The gold center works beautifully — it pops against both light and dark backgrounds, and the dual yellow/terracotta color scheme creates a warm, appetizing palette that says "gather around good food."
