My Recommendation: Deep Navy

If brown feels too "coffee shop", navy is the way to go. Gold + navy is a timeless, premium pairing. Think of luxury hotels, airline first class, high-end restaurants. It's sophisticated without being stuffy.

The navy contrasts beautifully with your warm cream content area while still feeling cohesive. And it makes the gold logo really pop.

Runner-up options: Charcoal (if you want neutral), Warm Black #1A1512 (matches your dark mode), or Deep Teal (if you want something more unique).
