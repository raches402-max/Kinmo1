Animation Notes

  • Movement: Each chair/arc slides inward from a scattered position to its final seat at the table
  • Stagger: Irregular timing (not evenly spaced) — feels like friends arriving naturally, not synchronized
  • Easing: Ease-out on each chair (fast start, gentle landing — like sliding a chair in)
  • Sound: Gentle chair-sliding sounds at each arrival, slightly overlapping
  • The reveal: When the last chair lands, the center circle pulses warmly — "Oh, it's a sun!"
  • Table brightens: The center circle starts dim and gets brighter as more friends arrive

The Metaphor

The static logo is the sun. But when you see the chairs pull in, you understand: friends gathering at a table creates warmth. The sun isn't just a sun — it's the feeling of everyone finally being together.

2. TABLE BRIGHTENS (0s - 1.8s)

3. WARM GLOW (1.4s)

Soft, natural sunlight radiates outward — not a ring, just gentle warmth spreading.

4. SETTLE (1.55s)

A gentle pulse — everyone's finally together.

Final Animation: "The Gathering"

Friends arrive at the table one by one, each at their own pace. When everyone's finally seated together, the shape reveals itself as a sun — and warmth radiates outward.

Design Process & Iterations

Iteration 1: Wrong shapes

Started with solid circles (dots) instead of the actual Kinmo logo. The logo uses arc/semi-circle shapes that represent chairs around a table — these are the key visual elements that needed to animate.

Iteration 2: Clockwise was too mechanical

Chairs arrived in clockwise order, which felt robotic and synchronized. Real friends don't arrive in perfect sequence — we needed randomness to feel natural.

Iteration 3: Ring glow was artificial

Tried expanding ring borders for the sun reveal. Looked like a digital effect, not natural warmth. Switched to radial gradients that fade smoothly — like actual light diffusing.

Iteration 4: Too fast

Animation was ~1.5 seconds total. Chairs felt "flung" into place, not pulled. Slowed everything down so each chair movement feels like someone actually pulling a chair to the table.

Iteration 5: Glow from center only

Glow was localized to the table center. But the metaphor is that the whole completed shape is the sun — table + chairs together. Expanded glow radius to encompass the entire logo.

The Metaphor

The static Kinmo logo is a sun. But when animated, you see the story: friends gathering at a table. Each chair slides in at its own pace — one friend arrives early, another strolls in late. The table gradually brightens as the group fills in. When the last friend finally takes their seat, you realize the shape you've been watching form is a sun. And then warmth radiates outward.

The sun isn't just a sun — it's the feeling of everyone finally being together. Friends gathering creates warmth.

Key Design Principles

Organic, not mechanical

Random arrival order. Varied speeds. No two chairs move identically. Like real humans.

Weighted movement

Chairs feel like they have mass. Deliberate, smooth easing — like pulling a real chair across a floor.

Natural warmth

Radial gradient glow, not hard-edged rings. Fades smoothly like actual light. The whole sun glows, not just the center.

Patience

~8 seconds is long for a logo animation. But the story needs time to breathe. Rushing undermines the metaphor.
