Recommendations

Best for small sizes (favicon): Concept 2 (4-seat) or Concept 3 (ring) — fewer elements stay readable at 16px


Most literal / storytelling: Concept 1 (6-seat) or Concept 5 (plate) — immediately communicates "gathering around food"


Most distinctive / brandable: Concept 4 (K-dots) — unique silhouette, memorable, but less literal about the dining aspect


My pick: Concept 2 (4-seat) strikes the best balance — it tells the story clearly, works at all sizes, and has a pleasing symmetry that feels warm and inviting.
