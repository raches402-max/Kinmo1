Design Notes

  • Search bar at top — Always visible, works across both tabs. Type "Blue Bottle" to find a specific place instantly.
  • "Liked by..." — Shows who in the group likes each venue. Creates social proof and fun discovery ("oh, Mike likes this place!").
  • "New for you" badges — AI-suggested venues interleaved with favorites. Visually distinct with dusty rose accent.
  • Swipe Mode buttons — Both tabs have them. From Favorites, you swipe through saved + new suggestions. From Discover, pure discovery mode.
  • Two tabs only — Simpler than 3 tabs. "Group Favorites" is primary (first), "Discover" is secondary.
  • Empty state — Gentle nudge to start discovering when no favorites exist.
